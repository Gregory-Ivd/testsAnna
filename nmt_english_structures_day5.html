Частина 1. Conditionals & wish — умовні речення

Оберіть правильну форму в умовному реченні (типи 0–3) або після wish / if only. (Q1–Q6)

  1. zero conditional If you heat ice, it _____ — that's basic physics.
  2. first conditional If it _____ tomorrow, we'll stay at home and watch a film.
  3. second conditional If I _____ a millionaire, I would travel all over the world.
  4. third conditional If she _____ harder, she would have passed the exam.
  5. wish (present) I wish I _____ how to drive — it would make life so much easier.
  6. if only (past) If only I _____ to her advice! I made a terrible mistake.

Частина 2. Modal verbs — модальні дієслова

Оберіть модальне дієслово за змістом (заборона, обов'язок, припущення, порада, здатність). (Q7–Q12)

  1. prohibition You _____ smoke here — it's a children's hospital.
  2. deduction (certain) He's been working since dawn; he _____ be exhausted by now.
  3. impossibility That _____ be Mark at the door — he's abroad on holiday right now.
  4. advice You look really pale. I think you _____ see a doctor.
  5. past ability When I was a child, I _____ swim across the whole lake without stopping.
  6. past modal (regret) You _____ told me about the meeting — now I've missed it completely.

Частина 3. Passive voice — пасивний стан

Оберіть правильну пасивну форму. Час «несе» дієслово to be, а смислове дієслово стоїть у V3. (Q13–Q18)

  1. present simple passive English _____ in more than fifty countries around the world.
  2. past simple passive This bridge _____ in 1995, long before I was born.
  3. present perfect passive Good news — the missing dog _____ and is already back home.
  4. modal passive All the reports _____ before the end of the week.
  5. passive + by The Mona Lisa _____ by Leonardo da Vinci in the early 16th century.
  6. present continuous passive You can't use the lift right now — it _____ at the moment.

Частина 4. Reported speech — непряма мова

Перетворіть пряму мову на непряму: зважай на «зсув часу» (backshift) і порядок слів. (Q19–Q24)

  1. backshift Direct: "I'm really tired." → She said that she _____ really tired.
  2. will → would Direct: "I will call you tonight." → He promised that he _____ call me that night.
  3. reported wh-question Direct: "Where do you live?" → She asked me where I _____.
  4. yes/no question Direct: "Do you like jazz?" → He asked me _____ I liked jazz.
  5. reported command Direct: "Open your books." → The teacher told us _____ our books.
  6. negative command Direct: "Don't touch the wires." → The engineer warned us _____ the wires.
